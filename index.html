Prices

The new M4 CS has seen some updates in weight as well as increased power. The use of carbon fiber reinforced plastic (CFRP) in many places, including the roof, saves 6 kg, while the hood is 25% lighter than in the M4. The fiery coupe has lost 32 kg in total as a result of his diet. Weight reduction efforts continue in the cabin. Using compact natural fibers in the rear section, the German manufacturer has made the M4 CS both minimalist and lightweight by reducing the climate control unit to a single stage. BMW engineers also updated the suspension system by overhauling the vehicle's shock absorber and spring settings. The new front splitter and rear diffuser in the M4 version, with light alloy wheels measuring 19 inches at the front and 20 inches at the rear, optimize the airflow passing under the vehicle. Thanks to all these updates, the BMW M4 CS managed to lap the Nürburgring in 7 minutes and 38 seconds. That makes it about 10 seconds slower than the M4 GTS. On the other hand, this rating is a full 14 seconds faster than the standard M4 with DCT transmission. BMW is asking €116,900 for the new M4 CS in Germany (about € 459,041 at today's rates) and £89,130 ​​(about € 419,020) in the United Kingdom. The Bavarian brand will start sales of the performance model before the second half of 2018.
